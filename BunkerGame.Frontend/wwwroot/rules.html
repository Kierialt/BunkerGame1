Game Rules

Round 1 Profession Reveal

Each player reveals only their Profession card. No other personal traits or characteristics are disclosed in this round.

Round 2+ Strategic Reveals

Players begin to strategically reveal their cards (health, hobbies, traits, etc.) to prove their value and convince others they deserve a spot in the bunker.

Voting & Elimination

At the end of each round, the group votes to eliminate one player. The least useful or most dangerous player is expelled from the bunker.

Game Goal

Survive the voting rounds and remain among the final group allowed into the bunker. Use your traits wisely, build alliances, and argue your case!
